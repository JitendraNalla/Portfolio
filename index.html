About me

I am Laser applications engineer with 3+ years of professional experience in laser applications, R&D and MSc in Advanced Manufacturing and Management. My work focuses on developing practical laser processes for complex materials and components, especially where accuracy, heat control and repeatability are critical.

I enjoy solving real manufacturing problems: improving process quality, reducing cycle time, developing ablation strategies, validating results through metrology, and communicating results clearly to customers and engineering teams.

Engineering expertise

A practical mix of laser processing, machine setup, inspection and process optimisation.

Laser drilling

Micro-hole drilling, taper reduction, circularity improvement, pitch strategy, focus control and cycle-time reduction.

Laser ablation

Femtosecond ablation, surface texturing, ceramic and metal removal, hatch strategy and low heat-affected processing.

Laser welding

CW and QCW fibre laser welding trials, fixture support, parameter optimisation and weld quality improvement.

Scanner systems

Galvo scanner process development, f-theta lens work, beam delivery, focus strategy and laserDESK toolpath preparation.

Metrology

Microscope inspection, SEM analysis, Alicona surface measurement, roughness evaluation and CMM/OGP comparison.

CAD/CAM

Siemens NX, SolidWorks, 2D/3D preparation, technical drawings, fixtures, patterns and manufacturing support.
